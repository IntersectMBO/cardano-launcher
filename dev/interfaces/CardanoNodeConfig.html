Optional delegationCertificate

delegationCertificate?: string
  • Defined in cardanoNode.ts:132

Path to the delegation certificate. The delegation certificate allows the delegator (the issuer of said certificate) to give his/her own block signing rights to somebody else (the delegatee). The delegatee can then sign blocks on behalf of the delegator.
